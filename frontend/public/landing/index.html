Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is WAG POS really free to start?
Yes! Our Starter plan is completely free forever. You get 2 users, 100 products, and basic reports. No credit card required. Upgrade only when you need more features.
Can I accept MTN Mobile Money payments?
Absolutely! WAG POS integrates with Paystack to accept MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money, and all major bank cards. Payments settle directly to your Ghanaian bank account.
Do I need special hardware?
No! WAG POS works on any device with a browser — laptop, tablet, or even your phone. For the best experience, we recommend a tablet or touchscreen monitor at your checkout counter.
Is my data secure?
Yes! We use bank-grade SSL encryption, JWT authentication, rate limiting, and input validation. Your data is stored on secure PostgreSQL databases with automated backups.
Can I use WAG POS for multiple shops?
Yes! Each shop gets its own unique URL and isolated data. You can manage multiple locations from one account. Perfect for chains and franchises.
